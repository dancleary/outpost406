Our Story

Mountain meets beach in this friendly Montana-themed outpost. Independently owned and operated, this relaxed pub offers cold craft beers on draft and a focused, "rustic comfort food" menu intended for sharing.

Sit back and kick your legs up in the "Porch View" section, watch the beachgoers stroll by while you sip ipa's and chow on Native American fry bread, or post up on a large communal table to watch your favorite teams on our wall-to-wall flat screens.

Friendly, open, no attitude pub with a Montana vibe. The "Last Best Place" now available by the pint!
